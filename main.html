Noodles

Noodles are a popular food enjoyed in many cultures around the world. They are usually made from flour and water, and sometimes eggs, which are mixed together to form a dough that is then rolled and cut into long, thin strands. Noodles can be cooked in many different ways, such as boiling, frying, or adding them to soups and stir-fries. They are very versatile because they can be served with vegetables, meat, or sauces, making them both tasty and filling. From Italian pasta to Asian ramen and stir-fried noodles, this simple food has become a favourite meal for people of all ages.
